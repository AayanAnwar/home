Death of a Shadow
#1

I woke up with a start, feeling a sharp pain in my head as well as my back.I noticed a heavy cloak on top of me (Is this mine? It doesn't... feel mine.). I tried to sit up, but as I did, I felt something soft on my chest. I looked down and noticed that on top of my heavy cloak, a few yellow, fluffy chicks were sleeping. I slowly laid back down.


Where am I?
I tried to make sense of my surrounding, so I looked around. I was in a sort of barn (there was hay on the floor), however, it was evident that there were no animals kept here, save for, perhaps, the tiny chicks.
The walls were made of wood, and there was a lantern atop me, as well as a few other beds made of lumps of hay. I realised that I was lying on one of these. There was a big window oppposite me (the kind with no glass), and I could see it was night-time.


How did I end up here? Why am I hurt? What happened?
I tried to recall, but nothing came to mind. I tried to remember as far back as I could, when I made a realization- I dont remember my name. What is going on? Why can't I-- "So you're finally awake?"


I sat straight up and turned around, I could feel the chicks chirping and running away. Standing before me was a man and a woman, but I can't see any of them clearly- it's too dark. "What happened to you?", the woman inquired.
I didn't say anything.
"What do you recall?"
"Nothing."
"What's your name?" I don't know...
She stares at me (I think she does- but I can't tell.).
"Do you remember anything?"
I shake my head.
She turns to man next to her, and whispers something to him. The woman walks forward to me. She's wearing a lab coat stained with a bit of red (blood?), and is around 5 foot. Her hair is tied in a messy bun.
"My name is Zulaya, and I was in charge of you while you were knocked out."
"What happened? Why am I here?"
"We were wondering the same thing."
"How did you find me? What's my name?", I say- I have many questions.
"It's best if you are not overloaded with information- you are recovering after all, and-"

"There was an explosion."
The icy voice travels through my body as the man steps forward.

The man is tall with pale skin and dark shoulder-length hair. He's wearing a half-black and half-white cloak, and layers of navy blue clothing underneath. He has two sheathed swords- one on his back, and one on the side of his hips. His eyes are covered with a sort of a strip of white gauze, and there is a ugly scar on the edge of his lip that has been tightly stitched together.
"This is Master Snake", Zulaya offers an uncomfortably long silence.
Even though his eyes are covered, he is looking straight at me.
"There was an explosion a few blocks over, from a suspected Deo*, in a wheat field. There was only one death, and you were found lying unconcious beside the deceased.
Do you really not remember anything?"
I shake my head again.
There is a short silence, broken when he says,"We didn't expect them to be prepared for you."
Who's we!?
"Who's we!?", I ask loudly this time.
"You... are a Shadow."
A what!?
"Our organization consists of Shadows like you, people whose job is to collect information about evey possible threat to this country, and counter any and all threats."
What is this man saying?
